# HIRO Programs

## Program Philosophy
Every HIRO program is designed for real adults with demanding lives. No 6 AM mandatory check-ins. No 2-hour workouts. No unsustainable restrictions. Just structured, progressive plans that deliver results while fitting into your actual schedule.

---

## The Foundation (12 Weeks)

**Description:** Our flagship comprehensive transformation program. Everything you need to transform your body, skin, and habits over 12 weeks.

**What's included:**
- 3x/week progressive strength training program (30-45 min)
- Full nutrition framework with meal templates and recipes
- Skincare routine builder
- Daily habit tracking and accountability
- Weekly check-ins and progress assessments
- Community support group

**Who it's for:** Members ready for complete transformation. Best for those who can commit to consistent effort over 12 weeks and want to build permanent habits.

**Equipment needed:** Dumbbells or resistance bands, or gym access

**Expected results:** Visible body composition changes, significant strength improvements, established skincare routine, consistent sleep and nutrition habits.

---

## The Kickstart (4 Weeks)

**Description:** A high-focus 4-week sprint to build momentum and establish the core HIRO habits. Perfect for getting started or resetting after a break.

**What's included:**
- 3x/week full-body workouts (25-35 min)
- Simple nutrition guidelines (no tracking required)
- Basic skincare routine
- Morning and evening habit checklist
- Daily motivation prompts

**Who it's for:** New members wanting a clear starting point. Experienced members returning after time off. Anyone who wants to prove to themselves they can be consistent for a month.

**Equipment needed:** Bodyweight only or minimal equipment

**Expected results:** 4-8 weeks of consistent habit formation, initial strength gains, improved energy and sleep, confidence from completing the commitment.

---

## The Executive (Ongoing)

**Description:** Designed for the busiest professionals — maximum results in minimum time. Efficient workouts, streamlined nutrition, and lifestyle optimization for those who can't spare a minute.

**What's included:**
- 2-3x/week hyper-efficient workouts (20-30 min)
- Grab-and-go nutrition strategies
- Sleep optimization protocol
- Stress management tools
- Travel-friendly routine adaptations
- Meeting-friendly movement breaks

**Who it's for:** CEOs, executives, entrepreneurs, parents of young children, frequent travelers, anyone working 60+ hours/week.

**Equipment needed:** Minimal (bodyweight, bands) with hotel-room-friendly options

**Expected results:** Maintained fitness levels despite schedule, improved energy management, better sleep quality, stress reduction, consistent health habits integrated into demanding lifestyle.

---

## The Reset (6 Weeks)

**Description:** A restorative program focused on recovery, mobility, and rebuilding fundamentals. Ideal after injury, burnout, or extended time away from fitness.

**What's included:**
- 3x/week mobility and functional movement sessions
- Anti-inflammatory nutrition protocol
- Sleep and recovery optimization
- Stress reduction practices
- Progressive return to strength work (weeks 4-6)

**Who it's for:** Members recovering from injury or illness. Those experiencing burnout or chronic fatigue. Anyone who has been sedentary and wants a safe return to activity.

**Equipment needed:** Foam roller, resistance bands, optional light dumbbells

**Expected results:** Improved mobility and movement quality, reduced pain and stiffness, restored energy levels, safe foundation for more intense training.

---

## The Skin Protocol (8 Weeks)

**Description:** A dedicated skincare transformation program that builds a complete, evidence-based routine for your specific skin type and concerns.

**What's included:**
- Personalized skincare routine builder
- Ingredient education (what works and why)
- Weekly routine adjustments based on skin response
- Nutrition for skin health guide
- Lifestyle factors that impact skin
- Product recommendations (HIRO and beyond)

**Who it's for:** Anyone wanting to transform their skin but feeling overwhelmed by options. Those who've tried products without results. Members wanting to age well and maintain healthy skin long-term.

**Expected results:** Clearer, more even skin tone, reduced fine lines and texture, established sustainable routine, confidence in choosing and using products.

---

## Program Selection Guide

**I want to lose weight and get in shape →** The Foundation

**I'm completely new to fitness →** The Kickstart → The Foundation

**I have no time →** The Executive

**I'm burned out / coming back from injury →** The Reset

**I want to focus on skin →** The Skin Protocol

**I travel constantly →** The Executive

**I want the full transformation →** The Foundation → ongoing maintenance

Multiple programs can be run simultaneously. For example, The Executive (fitness) + The Skin Protocol (skincare) is a common stack for busy professionals.
