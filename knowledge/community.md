# HIRO Community

## Our Community

HIRO is more than programs and products — it's a community of adults 30+ who are committed to becoming their best selves. We believe transformation happens better together.

## Community Values

### Real Over Perfect
Share the struggles, not just the wins. Real progress is messy and non-linear. The community values honesty about the journey.

### Support Over Judgment
Everyone is at a different stage. Some members are just starting. Others have been at this for years. All are welcome. All are respected.

### Evidence Over Hype
We value what works over what's trendy. If someone shares advice, it should be grounded in evidence or experience — not bro science or Instagram trends.

### Consistency Over Intensity
We celebrate daily discipline, not occasional heroics. The member who shows up 3x/week for a year has our respect more than the one who crushes a 30-day challenge and disappears.

## How the Community Works

### Discussion Areas
- **Daily Wins:** Share your small victories — the workout you almost skipped, the healthy choice you made, the progress you noticed
- **Questions & Advice:** Ask anything. Members and HIRO coaches respond with practical, experience-backed answers
- **Progress Journals:** Document your journey. The most inspiring content in HIRO is real members sharing real progress
- **Product & Program Feedback:** Honest reviews and experiences with HIRO products and programs

### Accountability
- Find an accountability partner with similar goals and schedule
- Weekly check-in threads help you stay on track
- Members often form small groups (3-5 people) for closer support

### Events and Challenges
- Monthly community challenges (step goals, consistency streaks, hydration)
- Live Q&A sessions with HIRO coaches
- Member spotlights celebrating outstanding progress

## Community Guidelines

1. Be respectful. Disagreement is fine. Disrespect is not.
2. No unsolicited selling or promotion of non-HIRO products
3. Medical advice: share experiences, not prescriptions. "This worked for me" is fine. "You should take this drug" is not.
4. Keep progress photos tasteful and focused on the transformation
5. What's shared in HIRO stays in HIRO — respect member privacy
6. Report concerns to community moderators, don't engage with trolls

## Getting the Most from Community

### If You're New
- Introduce yourself in the welcome thread
- Share your goals and what brought you to HIRO
- Don't be shy about asking questions — everyone started somewhere
- Find 2-3 members whose journey resonates and follow their progress

### If You're Experienced
- Your experience is valuable — share what you've learned
- Encourage newer members — you were in their shoes once
- Be specific in your advice ("I did X and it helped with Y" not "just work harder")
- Remember that what worked for you may not work for everyone

## The HIRO Difference

What makes the HIRO community different:
- **Age-appropriate:** Everyone here is 30+. The challenges, solutions, and conversations are relevant to your stage of life.
- **No toxicity:** No body shaming, no extreme diet culture, no "no days off" mentality. We support sustainable progress.
- **Multi-dimensional:** This isn't just a fitness community. Members discuss skincare, career, relationships, confidence — the full picture.
- **Action-oriented:** We're here to do the work, not just talk about it. The community inspires action, not just discussion.
