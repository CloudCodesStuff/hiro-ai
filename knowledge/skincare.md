# HIRO Skincare Knowledge

## Philosophy

Skincare at HIRO is about healthy, confident skin at any age. The focus is on effective routines that work with your lifestyle, not against it. No 12-step routines. No fear-based marketing. Just what actually works.

## Core Principles

### Consistency Over Complexity
- A simple routine done daily beats a complex one done occasionally
- 3-4 products used consistently is the sweet spot
- Results take 4-12 weeks — patience is essential

### Sun Protection is Non-Negotiable
- SPF 30+ every single day, regardless of weather
- UV exposure is responsible for 80% of visible skin aging
- Reapply every 2 hours when outdoors
- This is the single highest-impact skincare decision you can make

### Hydration From Inside and Out
- Water intake affects skin hydration
- Moisturizer seals in hydration
- Humidifier in dry environments helps significantly

## Basic Routine

### Morning
1. Cleanse (gentle, non-stripping)
2. Vitamin C serum (antioxidant protection)
3. Moisturizer
4. SPF 30+

### Evening
1. Double cleanse (oil cleanser + gentle foaming cleanser)
2. Treatment (retinoid or exfoliant — not both same night)
3. Moisturizer

## Key Ingredients by Concern

### Anti-Aging
- Retinoids (retinol, retinaldehyde, tretinoin): gold standard, start slow
- Peptides: support collagen production
- Vitamin C: antioxidant, brightens, supports collagen
- Niacinamide: improves skin barrier, reduces fine lines

### Hyperpigmentation / Dark Spots
- Vitamin C: brightens and prevents
- Azelaic acid: gentle, effective for most skin types
- Alpha arbutin: targets pigment production
- Tranexamic acid: effective for stubborn spots
- SPF: non-negotiable — treatments are useless without it

### Acne / Breakouts (yes, after 30 too)
- Salicylic acid (BHA): unclogs pores, 2-3x/week
- Benzoyl peroxide: kills acne bacteria, use as spot treatment
- Retinoids: regulate cell turnover long-term
- Niacinamide: reduces inflammation

### Dryness / Dehydration
- Hyaluronic acid: apply to damp skin
- Ceramides: repair skin barrier
- Squalane: lightweight, effective moisturizer
- Avoid hot water when washing face

## Age-Specific Notes (30+)

- Cell turnover slows — gentle exfoliation becomes more important
- Collagen production declines — retinoids and peptides help
- Hormonal changes can trigger adult acne
- Skin becomes drier — adjust moisturizer weight seasonally
- Eye area shows age first — dedicated eye cream can help

## Lifestyle Factors

### Sleep
- "Beauty sleep" is real — skin repairs at night
- 7-8 hours for optimal skin regeneration
- Sleep on your back to prevent sleep lines

### Nutrition for Skin
- Omega-3s reduce inflammation
- Vitamin C-rich foods support collagen
- Antioxidants (berries, green tea, dark chocolate) fight free radicals
- Limit sugar — it accelerates glycation (skin aging)
- Moderate alcohol — it dehydrates and inflames

### Stress
- Cortisol triggers breakouts and accelerates aging
- Stress management is skincare
- Exercise improves circulation to skin

## Common Mistakes

- Starting too many actives at once (irritation, barrier damage)
- Skipping SPF when using actives (increased sun sensitivity)
- Over-exfoliating (compromised barrier)
- Expecting overnight results
- Using products meant for 20-year-old skin
