# HIRO FAQ

## General

### What is HIRO?
HIRO is a premium glow-up and self-improvement community for busy adults 30+. We help members transform through fitness, nutrition, skincare, haircare, grooming, and lifestyle optimization — all built around realistic, sustainable approaches that fit into demanding lives.

### Who is HIRO for?
HIRO is for anyone 30+ who has spent years prioritizing career, family, and responsibilities and now wants to invest in themselves. If you want to improve how you look, feel, and perform — without extreme diets or unsustainable routines — HIRO is for you.

### Is HIRO for men or women?
HIRO is for everyone. Our approach works across all genders. The principles of fitness, nutrition, skincare, and lifestyle optimization are universal — we tailor the specifics to the individual.

### How is HIRO different from other fitness programs?
Most programs assume you have unlimited time, no responsibilities, and a 25-year-old's recovery capacity. HIRO is built for real adults with jobs, families, and busy schedules. We prioritize sustainability and integration into real life over extreme transformations.

## Results

### How long until I see results?
Most members notice improvements in energy and confidence within 2 weeks. Visible physical changes typically appear at 4-8 weeks, with significant transformation by 12 weeks. Consistency is the key variable.

### What if I'm not seeing results?
First: are you tracking the right things? Weight alone doesn't tell the story — measurements, photos, energy levels, and how clothes fit matter more. Second: nutrition typically needs attention before training. Third: sleep and stress can completely block progress.

### Is this a quick fix?
No. HIRO is for lasting transformation. Quick fixes fail 95% of the time. We teach sustainable systems that create permanent change.
